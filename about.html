Our Mission

Team DRIP is dedicated to minimizing the effects of diabetes in underserved communities, especially around wound care and mobility for diabetic amputees. We strive to improve access to education, assistive technologies, and healthcare resources.

Needs Statement

We aim to increase access to information on proper wound care and diabetes prevention to reduce unnecessary amputations. We're also developing affordable assistive walking devices for diabetic amputees who cannot afford commercial prosthetics.
