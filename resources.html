Foot Care & Prevention

These tips were previously on the Educational Pamphlet page and are kept here so everything is in one place.

  • Inspect your feet daily for cuts, blisters, redness, swelling, and nail issues.
  • Keep blood sugar in a healthy range and attend regular checkups.
  • Wear properly fitted footwear and avoid walking barefoot.
  • Stay active (as advised by your clinician) and check your feet after activity.
  • Don’t ignore numbness, tingling, or wounds that don’t heal.
